Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be 100% satisfied. Read our return policy here.

🗓️ 7-Day Replacement

We offer a 7-day hassle-free replacement policy for all defective or damaged products. If your product arrived damaged, please reach out to us within 48 hours of delivery with proof images/videos.

🔄 Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return or replacement, the product must be unused, in its original packaging, and include all accessories. Tag and seal should be intact.

💰 Refund Policy

Refunds are only issued if the product is out of stock for replacement or in case of non-delivery. Refunds will be credited to the original payment source within 5-7 business days of the return package being inspected.

❌ Non-Returnable Items

Items purchased on clearance sales or products showing signs of intentional damage/wear-and-tear are not eligible for returns.
