📖 How to Use

1. Open the lid and add 200ml of clean water.
2. Add 2-3 drops of your favorite essential oil (optional).
3. Connect the USB cable to a 5V power source.
4. Click the power button once for continuous mist, and long-press for LED light controls.

📥 Download User Manual (PDF)

🛠️ Troubleshooting Guide

Low Mist Output: Check if the water level is below the maximum limit and ensure the cotton swab is fully soaked before first use.
No Power: Check if the cable is connected properly and use a different power adapter if needed.
Noisy Operation: Ensure the humidifier is placed on a flat surface and that the water is clean.

🧼 Cleaning & Maintenance

To prevent mineral buildup, clean the water tank once a week with a mixture of water and vinegar. Avoid using harsh chemicals.
