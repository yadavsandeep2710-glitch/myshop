Shipping Information

Read about our shipping policies and delivery timelines below.

📦 Delivery Timelines

We typically ship within 24-48 hours of order confirmation. Our delivery partners usually deliver orders within 3-5 business days across Pan India. Some remote locations might take slightly longer.

🚛 Shipping Costs

We offer FREE shipping on all orders above ₹499. For orders below ₹499, a flat shipping fee of ₹50 will be applicable.

🔍 Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, we will share the tracking details on your provided phone number and email address via WhatsApp or SMS. You can track your package in real-time through our delivery partner's portal.

⚠️ Important Information

Please ensure that your shipping address and contact details are accurate to avoid delivery delays. In case of failed delivery attempts, our partners will try to contact you thrice before returning the package to our warehouse.
